BIO

After running away to Australia in 2012 and two years of traveling, I decided I wanted to build a website but had no idea how to do it. In 2014, I went to Martin College in Brisbane and got myself a diploma in Website design and Development. After becoming slightly obsessed with the tech industry, I decided to pack up my life I created on the Gold Coast and fly home to study Computing at Canterbury Christ Church University where I am soon to graduate.
